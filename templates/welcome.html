The Office of the County Attorney: Practice Sections

The Office of the County Attorney fulfills its mandate, as stipulated under Section 7 of the Office of the County Attorney Act, No. 14 of 2020, through eight specialized practice sections/divisions.

1. Dispute Resolution Practice Group/Area

Responsible for carrying out the following functions:

  • Representing and conducting all civil and constitutional cases instituted by and against the County Government.
  • Conducting or supervising Alternative Dispute Resolutions (ADR) and arbitration proceedings.
  • To provide legal expertise on the preparation, formulation, and litigation of civil cases.
  • Facilitating alternative dispute resolution for community matters and handling public interest litigation.
2. Legislative Drafting Practice Group/Area

Responsible for carrying out the following functions:

  • Drafting of proposed Bills, subsidiary legislation, and notices of appointments.
  • Examining and advising on draft Bills and Regulations through participation in meetings or written memoranda.
  • Attending to passage of Bills in the County Assembly and assisting in the preparation for assent by the Governor.
  • Publication of laws and subsidiary legislations in the Kenya Gazette.
3. County Government Transactions Practice Group/Area

Carries out the following functions:

  • Providing legal advice in negotiation, drafting, and vetting local and international contracts and MOUs.
  • Advising on Public Private Partnerships (PPPs) and project finance for infrastructure projects.
  • Undertaking conveyance transactions on behalf of the County Government.
  • Co-ordinates the County Attorney’s representation in Boards of County Corporations and Working Groups.
4. Legal Advisory & Research Services Practice Group/Area

Carries out the following functions:

  • Providing advisory services on a diverse range of substantive and procedural questions of law.
  • Advising the County Government on compliance with legislation and the Constitution.
  • Undertaking research for Government Departments on implementation of laws.
  • Overseeing the library, coordinating the pupillage program, and informing on current legal development.
5. County Law Office Development Practice Group/Area

Responsible for coordinating the proper functioning of all Divisions (Administrative & Support):

  • Manages departmental operations, administrative, and asset management issues.
  • Responsible for Capacity Building for all officers.
  • Comprises crucial support sections: Records, Finance, Accounts, HR, ICT, Internal Audit, Public Complaints, and Supply Chain Management.
6. Law Enforcement Practice Group/Area

Carries out the following functions:

  • Conducting and directing public prosecutions of breaches of penal provisions in county laws.
  • Prosecution and undertaking the enforcement of county laws, in liaison with the Office of the Director of Public Prosecutions (ODPP).
  • Conducting training of staff on county laws that have penal consequences.
  • Coordinating with county-based stakeholders (Judiciary and NPS) on enforcement of penal laws.
7. Legal Aid Clinics and Civic Education Practice Group/Area

Carries out the following functions:

  • Providing legal aid, advice, and pro bono services to the indigent, marginalized, and vulnerable persons.
  • Mediation and counseling in family matters and training in self-representation.
  • Sensitization of citizens through public participation and promotion of legal literacy and awareness.
  • Promoting the use of alternative dispute resolution methods that enhance access to justice.
8. Legal Audit and Compliance Practice Group/Area

Carries out the following functions:

  • Conducting annual trainings for various departments on applicable laws and regulations and ensuring compliance.
  • Reviewing the County policies, regulations, procedures, and Laws.
  • Assessing the performance of the County Government and its adherence to laws.
  • Analyzing internal governance structures to identify compliance gaps and improve overall performance.
